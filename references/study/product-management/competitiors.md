# Planable

1) supports a lot of social media platforms.  
2) Have multi level persona.  
3) can post videos on will after adding.

*1) No approval mechanism – in fact, Planable **does support multi-level approval workflows** with optional scheduling and locking upon approval.
*2) No framer mechanism.
*3) No versioning mechanism.
*4) No inbuilt communication mechanism – actually, Planable **does include internal comments, annotations, and feedback threads** for collaboration.

# Sendible

1) supports a lot of social media platforms.  
2) Have multi level persona.  
3) can post videos on will after adding.

*1) No approval mechanism – it **does have an approval workflow**, allowing messages/posts to be sent for approval within teams.
*2) No framer mechanism.
*3) No versioning mechanism.
*4) No inbuilt communication mechanism – actually, it **includes commenting in task approvals and content library collaboration**, plus communication via connected editors.

# SocialPilot

1) supports a lot of social media platforms.  
2) Have multi level persona.  
3) can post videos on will after adding.

*1) No approval mechanism – actually, it **does offer both internal team approval workflows** (pending review queue, manager/admin approval) **and client approval workflows**, including client portals and one-click approvals.
*2) No framer mechanism.
*3) No versioning mechanism.
*4) No inbuilt communication mechanism – it **does support internal comments and feedback**, including manager-to-scheduler comments and client notes.
